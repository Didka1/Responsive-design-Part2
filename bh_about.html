About our team

We are a little group of not so young people, but we believe that everybody deserves to be happy, every day of his life. We will try to make your day more colourful and happy with good ideas for events for your free time or with some advices about positive thinking.

What are our goals?

“We want to make the world a better place, but nobody gives us a source code!”

This is the label on the favorite T-shirt of our editor. And the truth is that we strongly believe that only you have the source code for your world and the key for your personal happiness. It is true that our days are dynamic and it is almost impossible to be positive in every situation. We don’t control every event in our lifes and that can be really scary, but there is a positive side we control our thoughts and that can be helpful. So… Our goal is to help you to make your thoughts more positive and to feel more satisfied and happy.

What can you find on our site?

We update our site every week and have weekly theme divided in six new pieces of information. We also have new advices and our editor’s opinion on a special topic. Every month we will provide information and reviews about new books and events. We hope that you will like our page!

And don't forget about...

...the positive thinking. It might help you to solve your problems and to make your live much more happy!
